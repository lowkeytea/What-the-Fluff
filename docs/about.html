About Us

Welcome to Lowkey Tea LLC! Founded by an enthusiast encouraged by his family to spread his wings, the focus of Lowkey Tea is to create fun, engaging experiences on mobile devices making use of the latest technologies. Our first app, What the Fluff!? News! is a news app that is just that - a fun RSS reader that has various AI personalities to choose from to read the news or just chat with you. Our mission is simple: create unique experiences and get back to the basics of what makes a good app great.
